About Me

I am a mathematician, currently working as a Senior Research Officer at the Malaria Atlas Project (MAP) in Perth Western Australia. Prior to this I was a Postdoctoral Research Associate at the University of Oxford working on mathematical models of ice-sheet dynamics. I also completed my DPhil (PhD) in Industrially Focussed Mathematical Modelling at the University of Oxford. Before moving to the UK I completed my undergraduate degree in Mathematics and Physics (honours) as part of the Advanced Science program at the University of Western Australia.

I built this website to share my work and some of my interests. This is still in development and I'm still moving information from my old website to this one. If you wish to get in touch, please email me at michael.mcphailremove-this@thekids.org.au.

The most important thing to know about my blog is that it isn't really a blog. I don't know how to blog; I don't think I'm well suited to blogging; and, to be frank, I'm not really interested in blogging. The tone of my writing will resemble a blog (mostly conversational), and I aim to update regularly; however, my posts are unlikely to be static. I will be updating each post if I have more to say, or find out something else, or can think of a better way to say something. The dates on each post will represent the date of the most recent update, but not always the date the post was first published. I can't imagine ever being satisfied with my first draft/version, and I don't think I'll have have comprehensive understanding of these complicated topics after just one pass over. 

I'm not a prolific writer. I find that trying to write well is tiring, if not impossible when things are busy. As such, I don't expect this blog to be updated regularly. 

This feed will mostly cover topics I've recently taken an interest in and want to memorialise.

## What to Expect
Most of the posts here will fall under broad "series" categories (*e.g.*, Earth Observation, Transport Newtork Models,...). If an topic has interested me, I would like to flesh it out over the course of multiple posts, hopefully in some structured manner that breaks big complex topics into bite-sized chunks.
